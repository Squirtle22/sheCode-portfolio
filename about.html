About Me

WebDeveloper from Wiltshire, UK

Hello, I'm Deanna Keel, a passionate junior web developer on a mission to transition from a rewarding career in childcare to the dynamic world of web development.

My journey into web development began with a desire for a new challenge and a fascination with the endless possibilities that the digital world offers. While my background in childcare provided me with valuable skills in patience, empathy, and effective communication, I yearned for a career that would allow me to blend creativity with problem-solving.

In my work, I strive for attention to detail, a user-centric mindset, and a commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest web technologies and best practices. I'm a strong believer in continuous learning, and I eagerly embrace challenges as opportunities for growth. Whether it's coding, designing, or troubleshooting, I'm always excited to tackle new projects and expand my horizons.
